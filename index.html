Our Mission

Samanwaya is a youth-led organization driving democratic processes, political coordination, and civic empowerment across Nepal. We believe in building a stronger democracy through active participation and engagement.

Youth Empowerment

Empowering young leaders to take active roles in democratic processes and community development.

Women's Rights

Advocating for women's participation in politics and decision-making processes.

Children's Welfare

Ensuring children's voices are heard and their rights are protected in our democracy.

Civic Engagement

Promoting meaningful civic participation and political coordination across Nepal.
