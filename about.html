About Samanwaya

Building a Stronger Democracy Through Youth Leadership

Our Vision

Samanwaya envisions a Nepal where youth, women, and children are active participants in democratic processes and decision-making. We believe that meaningful civic engagement is the foundation of a strong and resilient democracy.

Our Mission

To empower youth, women, and children through democratic processes, political coordination, and meaningful civic engagement. As a youth-led organization, we drive democratic processes, political coordination, and civic empowerment across Nepal.

What We Do

Youth Empowerment: We provide platforms and opportunities for young people to develop leadership skills, engage in policy discussions, and take active roles in their communities.

Women's Rights: We advocate for gender equality and work to increase women's participation in politics and governance at all levels.

Children's Welfare: We ensure that children's voices are heard and their rights are protected in our democratic processes.

Civic Engagement: We organize workshops, seminars, and community events to promote political awareness and civic participation among citizens.

Our Approach

Samanwaya believes in inclusive, participatory democracy. We work through:

  • Political coordination and dialogue
  • Community-based programs and initiatives
  • Educational workshops and training sessions
  • Advocacy campaigns for policy change
  • Partnerships with like-minded organizations

Join Us

We welcome individuals who share our vision of a stronger, more inclusive democracy. Whether you're a young person looking to make a difference, a woman seeking to amplify your voice, or a concerned citizen wanting to contribute to positive change, Samanwaya offers opportunities for everyone.
